FACIAL ERASER CLEANSING OIL

size : 200 ml /6.76 fl.oz

DETAIL

Lightweight formula cleansing oil to remove make-up.

Formula

Translucent oil formulation that immediately turns into emulsion form when exposed to water

GO SHOP

KEY INGREDIENTS

Agar

Agar with its evenly fine tissues absorbs the find dust and external contaminants and supplies moisture to the skin. *Agar: Gelidium Cartilagineum

Coconut Oil

Coconut oil that melts makeup gently and removes makeup thoroughly by cleansing once.

Salicornia herbacea

Exceptional hydration to leave the skin moisturized.

HOW TO USE

Pump about 2 times on your palm and gently circle over face to massage. When the makeup residues and impurities melt, rinse several times with lukewarm water.

Mineral oil, Isopropyl palmitate, Pentaerythrityl tetraethylhexanoate, C12-15 alkyl benzoate, Pentaerythrityl tetraisostearate, Caprylic/capric triglyceride, Peg-20 glyceryl triisostearate, Peg-8 isostearate, Dicaprylyl carbonate, Cocos nucifera (coconut) oil, Oxygen, Gelidium cartilagineum extract, Alteromonas ferment extract, Melaleuca alternifolia (tea tree) leaf oil, Salicornia herbacea extract, Dextrin myristate, Dextrin palmitate, Butylene glycol, Water, Coco-caprylate/caprate, Caprylyl glycol, Fragrance
