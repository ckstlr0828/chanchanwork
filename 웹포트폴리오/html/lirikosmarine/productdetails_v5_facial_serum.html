KEY INGREDIENTS

Multi-Vitamins

The harmony of 10% multi-vitamins and brown algae extracts. Lirikos Marine Energy Team has developed the original formula of Vitamin B5 Derivatives (Pantethine/D-Panthenol), Vitamin B3, Vitamin C Derivatives, Vitamin E, and brown algae extracts. *Vitamin B3: helps with skin brightening. *Vitamin E has antioxidant function and protects the skin from external environment. *Brown Algae Extract helps with resilience.
