Light-weight gel creamn

KEY INGREDIENTS

Deep Sea Water

Deep Sea Water in Lirikos Marine Energy is the clean water rich in minerals from more than 200 m underwater away from the sunlight, bacteria, and pollution. It fills one's dry skin with moisture and makes your skin look revitalized.

Seaweed Ferment

Helps with moisturization and skin's stress relief. *Seaweed Ferment: Alteromonas Ferment Extract

HOW TO USE

Evenly apply on the face starting from the center and working outward, gently pat with your fingers to promote absorption into the skin.
