KEY INGREDIENTS

Korean West Sea Mud

It is the pearl mud gathered from the wetlands of Korean West Sea that have been piled in layers for thousands of years. The wetlands have created and cherished the vitality of ocean for a long time and condensed the powerful and clean energy of ocean. The West Sea is known for its shallow waters and condensed mud containing various living plankton and algae. The mud( in Hot Melting Mud Mask) adheres to the skin with fine and even particles.

Scrub

The walnut shell powder gently exfoliates the skin.

HOW TO USE

After cleansing, apply a thick layer over dry face, avoiding the eye and lip areas.

Glycerin, Sea silt, Butylene glycol, Zeolite, Kaolin, Polyglyceryl-10 behenate/eicosadioate, Silica, Titanium dioxide (ci 77891), Hydroxyethyl acrylate/sodium acryloyldimethyl taurate copolymer, Juglans regia(walnut) shell powder, Ethylhexylglycerin, Sorbitan isostearate, Polysorbate 60, Water / aqua / eau, Tocopherol, Phenoxyethanol, Alteromonas ferment extract, Disodium edta
