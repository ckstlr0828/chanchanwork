FACIAL ERASER CLEANSING BALM

size : 90 g/ 3.17 NET.wt

DETAIL

It is a Cleansing Balm wiping off make-up and even dust sticking on the skin.

Formula

Balm formulation like sherbet turns into cleansing oil when it is applied on the skin.

GO SHOP

KEY INGREDIENTS

Agar

Agar with its evenly fine tissues absorbs the find dust and external contaminants and supplies moisture to the skin.
*Agar: Gelidium Cartilagineum

Tea Tree Oil

It soothes the skin for soothing and purifying effect.

HOW TO USE

Using the spatula, dispense an appropriate amount onto a clean palm and apply on dry face. Gently massage the face, and then wash off with lukewarm water.

INGREDIENTS

Cetyl ethylhexanoate, Hydrogenated poly(c6-14 olefin), Peg-20 glyceryl triisostearate, Caprylic/capric triglyceride, Isopropyl palmitate, Polyethylene, Silica, Peg-8 isostearate, Alteromonas ferment extract, Oxygen, Gelidium cartilagineum extract, Melaleuca alternifolia (tea tree) leaf oil, Water, Butylene glycol, Caprylyl glycol, Fragrance
