HYDRO PEELING CREAM

size : 50 ml/ 1.69 fl.oz

DETAIL

The hydro peeling cream that exfoliates and moisturizes at the same time to enhance the condition of your skin.

Formula

Formulations that grow extensively and stick to the skin

GO SHOP

KEY INGREDIENTS

AHA Salt

The AHA in the form of salt exfoliates more gently than AHA with deeper hydration. It leaves the skin free of dryness and smooth.

Protease Enzyme

Lirikos' Marine Energy Team has studied the microbes growing in the wetlands of the West Coast to develop the protease enzymes for exfoliation. Protease enzymes exfoliate the dead skin cells to promote the skin turnover and leave your skin softer and smoother.

Glycerin and Squalane

It Contains 17% moisturizing ingredients, such as plant-origin glycerin and Squalane to give moisturizing relief.

HOW TO USE

Take a proper amount of content and gently apply it from the center of the face toward the outside. If you take a sleep after using it in the finishing phase, you can experience skin that is smoother the next day.
