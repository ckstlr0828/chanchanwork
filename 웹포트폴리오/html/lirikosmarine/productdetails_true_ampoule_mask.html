TRUE AMPOULE MASK

size : 23 ml * 1sheet/ 0.77 fl.oz*1 sheet

DETAIL

The ampoule sheet mask, which is enriched with precious marine-origin ingredients, helps to moisturize the skin.

Formula

The sheet adheres to the skin to deliver the effect of the serum.

KEY INGREDIENTS

Glasswort

Salicornia herbacea, the cactus of ocean that absorbs the moisture of wetlands! The rich nourishment in Salicornia herbacea leaves the skin healthy and smooth. The Salicornia herbacea extracts from the seeds found in Korean seas protect and emulsify the skin for healthier, rejuvenated-looking skin.

Deep Sea Water

Deep Sea Water in Lirikos Marine Energy is the clean water rich in minerals from more than 200 m underwater away from the sunlight, bacteria, and pollution. It fills one's dry skin with moisture and makes your skin look revitalized.

Pearl

Pearls are known as God's gift for the mankind. They are rich in pearl protein and various organic substances to deliver natural radiance to the skin.

Oyster

Oysters are called milk of the sea. It provides rich nutrition to skin and makes the skin look shiny.

HOW TO USE

1. Apply toner after cleansing the face.
2. Take the mask out from the pouch and evenly apply the sheet on the entire face avoiding the eye and mouth areas.
3. Leave the mask on for 10 to 20 minutes then peel off. Gently pat the remaining essence into the skin.
