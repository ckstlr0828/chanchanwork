FACIAL ERASER CLEANSING FOAM

size : 150 ml/ 5.07 fl.oz

DETAIL

The deep cleansing foam makes the skin clean by removing impurities accumulated on the skin with its dense foam and fine scrub.

Formula

Cleansing foam that forms densed bubble with fine scrubs

GO SHOP

KEY INGREDIENTS

Agar

Agar with its evenly fine tissues absorbs the find dust and external contaminants and supplies moisture to the skin. *Agar: Gelidium Cartilagineum

Kaolin

Kaolin is great at absorbing excessive sebum and impurities and leaves the clarified-looking skin.

HOW TO USE

Dispense an appropriate amount onto a clean palm and fully lather. Gently massage the face and wash off with lukewarm water.
