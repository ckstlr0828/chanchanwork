HYDRATING PULSE ESSENCE LOTION

size : 150 ml/ 5.07 fl.oz

DETAIL

The light and fresh formula of water-like essence type keeps the skin comfortable and moisturized.

Formula

Though it is a dilute formulation like essence, it is quickly absorbed at the moment of application.

GO SHOP

KEY INGREDIENTS

Ascophyllum Nodosum

It is extracted from the brown algae rich in minerals and polyphenone. There are many air pockets on the leaves and its lifespan is 10 to 15 years. Ascophyllum Nodosum Extracts help to moisturize the skin and prevent moisture from being taken from the outside environment.

Seaweed Ferment

Helps with moisturization and skin's stress relief.
*Seaweed Ferment: Altromonas Ferment Extract

HOW TO USE

Evenly apply on the face starting from the center and working outward, gently pat with your fingers to promote absorption into the skin.
