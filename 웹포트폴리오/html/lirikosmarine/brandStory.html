Brand Story

LIRIKOS MARINE ENERGY WANTS TO
DELIVER THE SECRET OF MARINE ENERGY.

The source of vitality contained in marine plant is a fermented seaweed component created from the symbiosis between seaweed and microorganisms. We call this amazing secret ingredient Marine Energy™. Marine Energy™ is the essence of Lirikos Marine Energy, which further specializes the seaweed’s excellent effect on skin.

MARINE ENERGY HAS
BEGUN FROM THE SECRET OF THE SEA’S ENERGY.

Since a long time ago, female divers who have been gathering seafood in the salty seawater under the hot sunlight pick one strand of seaweed, and attach it onto their skin when their skin starts feeling burned. Surprisingly, their skin is soothed immediately, even looking more smooth and radiant. Come to think of it, seaweeds always have a smooth texture. Even if they are rubbed by rough waves hither and thither, they are damaged but they remain moist and elastic. “Wouldn’t it be great if we can deliver this surprising energy of marine plants into the skin in its entirety?” Thus begun LIRIKOS Marine Energy’s research on the powers of algae.

MARINE ENERGY™ WANTS TO DELIVER THE SECRET OF MARINE ENERGY.

The source of vitality contained in marine plant is a fermented seaweed component created from the symbiosis between seaweed and microorganisms. Lirikos's long hours of study to extract and optimize this component on human skin has come to fruition. We call this amazing secret ingredient Marine Energy™. It enhances the skin's strength based on moisture, skin stress relief and skin barrier enhancement effects. Marine Energy™ is the essence of Lirikos Marine Energy, which further specializes the seaweed’s excellent effect on skin.

MARINE LAB HAS EXPLORED THE SECRET OF MARINE ENERGY.

Marine Lab has put in advanced technology and long-term efforts in order to discover the source of algae’s strong vitality and how it works effectively to the skin. The researchers of Marine Lab seek to bring changes to the skin by deciphering the secrets of the sea. They are exploring the secrets of the sea even at this moment.
